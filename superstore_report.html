Key Business Insights
🪑

Furniture Is a Profit Drain

With $2.66M in sales but -$193K in profit, Furniture has a deeply negative margin. Aggressive discounting is likely the culprit. Immediate review of discount policies is critical.

💻

Technology Leads Profitability

Technology products generate $137K in profit from $2.99M in sales (~4.6% margin). Phones, Copiers, and Accessories are the star performers — worth prioritizing in marketing.

📦

Office Supplies: High Margin, Low Revenue

Office Supplies delivers the healthiest margin (~7.1%) despite lower revenue. Scaling this category through targeted promotions and bundle deals could significantly lift profits.

🏷️

Discounts Above 20% Destroy Profits

The discount vs. profit scatter shows that any order with a 20%+ discount becomes unprofitable. A cap on maximum discount levels is strongly recommended.

🌎

West Region is the Revenue Leader

The West region dominates with $2.14M in sales. However, South shows the best profit margin, suggesting West may be over-discounting to drive volume.

📈

Strong Q4 Seasonality

Sales spike consistently in Q4 across all years. Pre-positioning inventory and marketing budgets for October–December could amplify this seasonal opportunity.

Strategic Recommendations
1

Cap Discounts at 20% Across All Categories

Implement a hard ceiling on discount rates at 20%. Orders above this threshold are consistently loss-making. Use A/B testing to evaluate whether lower discounts maintain conversion rates.

2

Conduct Full Furniture Category Audit

Furniture's negative profitability demands immediate action. Review cost structure, pricing strategy, and discount policies for Tables, Bookcases, and Chairs specifically.

3

Scale Office Supplies via Bundle Promotions

With the best profit margins, Office Supplies deserves more revenue focus. Create bundles, cross-sell with Technology, and launch targeted campaigns in corporate and home office segments.

4

Double Down on Technology Star Products

Phones, Accessories, and Copiers are best-in-class profit generators. Allocate larger marketing budgets, negotiate better supplier terms, and ensure strong inventory levels heading into Q4.

5

Implement Regional Discount Governance

East region's near-negative margin suggests regional managers may be using excessive discounts to hit volume targets. Introduce margin-based performance KPIs alongside revenue targets.
